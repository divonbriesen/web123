Extra Credit: Your Own Domain (M17)

Have your own domain? Deploy your whole set of sites to it and earn extra credit. A domain, not a subdomain — yourname.com counts, yourname.github.io or yourname.wordpress.com does not.

How

  • The easiest path: buy a domain (~$12 at GoDaddy, Namecheap, or a registrar of your choice) and attach it to your github.io account — your pages are already there, and updates keep flowing through the same repo.
  • The GitHub Student Developer Pack offers free domains on select TLDs — but domain freebies are often not worth the headache (limited choices, or free-then-you-pay).
  • A great, easy way to show off a resume or host a future project.

Submitting

  • The URL of your personal page as it exists now.
  • The URL of the same page on your own domain.
  • Remember: a subdomain does not count.
  • Peer reviews here also earn extra credit.
