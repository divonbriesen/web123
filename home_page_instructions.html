Home Page Instructions

The main page of your webspace — both at webpages.charlotte.edu/youruserid/ and yourgithubid.github.io — is your personal home page. It's about YOU; the course site is about the COURSE. Anyone landing on your subdomain sees this page and nothing course-specific beyond one link.

Requirements

  • An embedded stylesheet does the styling (it's a single page — no linked local CSS).
  • Both validators on the page, all standards apply, and check WCAG manually.
  • The CLT and GitHub Pages copies match each other.

Submitting
