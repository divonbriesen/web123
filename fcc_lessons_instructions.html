freeCodeCamp Lesson Assignments (M2, M3, M04, M7A)

Four assignments track your progress through the freeCodeCamp Full Stack Curriculum. Do the work logged into the account you submitted in M0e — grading cross-references your public profile, and completion is checked per module, including the lectures, quizzes, labs, and reviews inside each (not just the numbered steps).

  • Semantic HTML — 39 steps
  • Forms and Tables — 59 steps
  • Accessibility — 24 steps

M04 — FCC4: CSS Continued

M7A — Responsive Web Design Certification

  • Complete the RWD certification on freeCodeCamp (the projects and the exam).
  • Add a link to your certification in your course site footer — the URL looks like freecodecamp.org/certification/yourusername/responsive-web-design-v9.

Submitting (each assignment)

  • Your public FCC profile URL — points, timeline, and activity visible.
  • A single cropped screenshot showing the listed modules with completion checkmarks, your name visible somewhere (like in the URL bar).
  • Notes, and names of anyone you worked with.
  • Peer reviews: apply the instructions to at least two classmates who don't already have two reviews.

What gets checked: every module in the assignment shows 100% of its challenges completed on your profile — quizzes, labs, and reviews included. For M7A: the certification is earned and your course footer links to it.
