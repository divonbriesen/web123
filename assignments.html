Assignments

Instructions, samples, and tools for the recurring assignments. Each one is also expected to follow the Web Standards and, on your course site, the Course Site Standards.

Ongoing Discussions/Journals/Logs

  • Journal Instructions — twice-weekly reflective posts and replies.
  • Work Log Instructions — log every 15+ minutes of work with time, location, and tasks.
  • General Discussion Instructions — where to ask questions you actually want answered.

Introductions

  • Introductions Instructions — the required steps, content, and formatting for the shared doc.
  • Intro Samples — four complete sample entries to model yours on.
  • Annotated Intro — one sample with every formatting rule labeled in place.
  • Introduction Evaluation Tool — paste your intro for a pre-submission check.

Building & Grading

  • First HTML — the step-by-step first pages sequence.
  • Grading Tool — how submissions get evaluated.
