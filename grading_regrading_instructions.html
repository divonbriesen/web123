Grading/Regrading Issues (M00)

Canvas is less than ideal at telling the TAs and instructor when something needs review — a problem in a class with multiple iterations per assignment. This board is for misunderstandings or regrades you think we've missed.

Before You Post Here

  • First priority: reply to the existing feedback — generally on the discussion forum itself — with something like "I've fixed this," or a list of what you fixed. This board is for when that doesn't work or something else is going on.
  • No feedback yet? That means we haven't graded your item yet — don't post here about it (unless you think we somehow missed the entire assignment for the whole class).

How to Post

  • The name of the assignment
  • A link directly to your submission
  • Any explanation you care to include

It may not be necessary to post here at all — that's a good thing.
